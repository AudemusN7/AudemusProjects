A GS year has 10 months in the pattern 34, 35, 34, 34, 35, 34, 35, 34, 34, 35. Every 34th GS year is an anti-leap year, so the final month has 34 days. A GS day is 20 hours, each hour has 100 minutes, each minute has 100 seconds, and each GST second is half a UTC second. Across the anti-leap cycle, the average GS year is 398.114 UTC days.
